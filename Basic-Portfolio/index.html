About Me

I popped out of my mother on May 3rd,1995 born in

New Jersey raised here as well. I wasn't a big fan of

school growing up but I loved learning so whenever

something has my attention it's 100% concentration.

Finally getting into coding was about three months

prior when in the middle of the summer my friend

was talking to me about how he was taking an off year from school and mentioned

bootcamps. I had no idea what coding was when he first mentioned it only that I've

played around with html and I didn't even realize it was code until later on. After

doing my research it seemed to grab my attention enough to try it out, finishing a

couple online courses had taken my interest to the next level wanting to have a

cirriculum structured around my education of code. That is how I ended up at

Rutgers Coding Bootcamp today. As far as outside of code I bike 3 miles a day to

and from work to keep me active, I'll play any sport with a friend. Reading and Art

seems to entertaint me more now a days then television/games/movies, to keep up

to date with these current events in art/tech I use Medium, Thenextweb, and

Hypebeast.
